About us

Freakyline is a real-time, 24-hours-a-day business news and commentary website based in India established in 2021. Freakyline provides prompt and quality information for investors of Indian and US Stock Markets. Freakyline forecast stock target price and give unbiased judgement on different institutional firm’s stock price. Website cover’s each and every dominant news of Indian and US stock market.

Freakyline is designed to help people in business, finance, government, the professionals as well as investors. Our Cryptocurrency News feed is a one stop shop destination on all the latest news in crypto, we offers original coverage of the global blockchain and cryptocurrency news, provides unbaised opinions, reviews, guides, introduces to Crypto people with an aim to help the general public. Freakyline is free for everyone and is funded by advertising revenue. Our main intension is to provide quality knowledge to our audience free of cost.
